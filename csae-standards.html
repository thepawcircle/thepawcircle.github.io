The Paw Circle Standards Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

Effective date: April 15, 2026

The Paw Circle has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), including child sexual abuse material (CSAM), grooming, sexual extortion, and any content that sexualizes minors.

These standards apply to all users and all content shared through The Paw Circle.

1) Prohibited Content and Behavior

The following are strictly prohibited:

  • Any child sexual abuse material (CSAM), including real, manipulated, or synthetic content.
  • Any sexual content involving minors or content that appears to depict minors in a sexual context.
  • Grooming behavior, including attempts to befriend or manipulate a minor for sexual purposes.
  • Sexual extortion, trafficking-related behavior, or coercion involving minors.
  • Requests for, sharing of, or links to CSAE content on or off platform.
  • Any attempt to use The Paw Circle to facilitate CSAE.

2) Detection, Moderation, and Enforcement

We use moderation processes to detect and act on abusive content and behavior, including user reports and internal review.

When we identify potential CSAE violations, we may:

  • Remove content immediately.
  • Suspend or permanently ban accounts.
  • Preserve relevant data as required for legal and safety purposes.
  • Report credible CSAE activity to appropriate law enforcement and child safety authorities, where required by law.

3) How to Report CSAE Content

If you encounter content or behavior that may involve child sexual abuse or exploitation, report it immediately:

  • In-app: use available reporting tools (if present in your app version).
  • Email: thepawcircle.blr@gmail.com

When reporting, include:

  • A description of the content or behavior.
  • Profile, post, or other identifying details (if available).
  • Any relevant timestamp or screenshot.

Do not share suspected CSAM further.

4) Cooperation With Authorities

The Paw Circle cooperates with valid legal requests and applicable reporting obligations related to CSAE. We may provide relevant account and content information as required by law.

5) Child Safety Commitment

We continuously review and improve our safety controls, moderation standards, and response processes to help prevent misuse of our platform.

6) Contact

For child safety concerns:

  • Safety contact: thepawcircle.blr@gmail.com
  • Developer/App: The Paw Circle

If this contact address changes, this policy page will be updated.

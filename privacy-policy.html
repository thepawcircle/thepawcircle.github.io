Privacy Policy for The Paw Circle

Effective date: April 15, 2026

The Paw Circle ("we", "our", or "us") provides a mobile app that helps people document and support stray animals in their community. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

By using The Paw Circle, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1) Information We Collect

a) Information you provide

  • Phone number: used to sign in using one-time password (OTP) verification.
  • Profile information: such as display name and optional profile photo.
  • User content: photos and descriptions you upload when reporting or updating stray profiles.

b) Information collected from device permissions

  • Camera access (CAMERA): to capture photos in-app for stray reports and updates.
  • Photo library access (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / photo picker): to choose existing photos from your device.
  • Location access (ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION, ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION): to show nearby stray reports, support map features, and display location context.

You can manage permissions in your device settings.

c) Automatically collected information

  • Basic technical and usage data needed to operate the app, such as app version, timestamps, and API request metadata.
  • Authentication/session tokens used to keep you signed in securely.

2) How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Authenticate your account via OTP.
  • Provide core app features (reporting strays, viewing profiles, uploading photos, map/location features).
  • Show your public contribution details in the community (for example, uploaded photos and earned badges).
  • Improve app performance, reliability, and safety.
  • Detect and prevent abuse, fraud, and misuse.

3) Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information only in these cases:

  • Service providers that help operate the app (for example, infrastructure, SMS/OTP delivery, mapping/location services), under appropriate safeguards.
  • Legal compliance when required by law, regulation, legal process, or valid government request.
  • Safety and enforcement to protect users, animals, our services, and legal rights.

Content you post in community-visible areas (such as stray photos and related profile data) may be visible to other users according to app functionality.

4) Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Retention periods may vary by data type (for example, account records vs. media uploads). We may delete or anonymize data when no longer required.

5) Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6) Children's Privacy

The Paw Circle is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children below the applicable age threshold.

If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7) Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access or request a copy of your personal information.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal and operational requirements.
  • Withdraw permissions (such as camera/location) via device settings.

To make a privacy request, contact us at the email below.

8) International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than where you live, where our service providers operate. We take reasonable steps to protect data consistent with applicable law.

9) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version with a new effective date.

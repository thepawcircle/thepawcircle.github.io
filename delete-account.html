Account Deletion

How to Delete Your Account

We understand that you may want to delete your account. Please follow the steps outlined below.

Step 1: Log In

Ensure you are logged into your account.

Step 2: Navigate to Account Settings

Go to the account settings section of the website.

Step 3: Choose Account Deletion

Select the option to delete your account. Follow the prompts to proceed.

Important Information

Please be aware that deleting your account is irreversible. All your data will be permanently removed.

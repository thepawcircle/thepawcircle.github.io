Delete Your The Paw Circle Account

This page explains how to request deletion of your The Paw Circle account and associated data.

How to request account deletion

Send an email to thepawcircle.blr@gmail.com from the phone number-linked email account (or include your registered phone number in the request):

  • Subject: Delete my The Paw Circle account
  • Include:
    • Your registered phone number (with country code, for example +91XXXXXXXXXX)
    • Optional: any additional account details that help us verify ownership

We may request a verification step to protect your account from unauthorized deletion requests.

What data is deleted

After successful verification, we delete or anonymize:

  • Your account profile data
  • Auth/session data related to your account
  • User-generated profile content associated to your identity where deletion is technically and legally feasible

What may be retained, and for how long

We may retain limited records for legal, abuse-prevention, security, audit, or dispute-resolution reasons.
Such retained data is kept only as long as required by applicable law or legitimate operational need, then deleted or anonymized.

Timeline

We aim to process valid deletion requests within 30 days.
